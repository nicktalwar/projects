Soundlane

6/2013—9/2013, iOS.

Soundlane sought to make consuming text via text-to-speech a seamless transition from desktop to auto. From this product vision, a car-optimized, content-browsing UX was borne to enable this consumption.

The visual design language here takes cues from life on the open road and the created brand, Soundlane. Additionally, all interactions are gesture driven, letting the driver maintain most of their attention on the road rather than fumbling for small touch-points. Over time, learned gestures would create an eyes-free experience.

To accomodate those commuting via public transport, one could toggle to a more content-dense browse interaction as need be.







Responsive site coded in HTML5/CSS3/jQuery with the Bootstrap framework by Nick Talwar. All rights reserved.
