Urbandipity

11/2008—8/2011, web.

Urbandipity was a social web app that enabled people to meet-up easily based on common interests. I led product, business development, and marketing efforts (among other things) as a cofounder. Our successful launch in partnership with Seattle's largest publication, Seattle Weekly, happened in early 2011.

Over three years, we formulated the product, making successful pivots, and surviving a cease-and-desist to reach product-market fit.

The best short product synopsis was our interview in GeekWire. We also produced a video landing page intro that converted well. If you'd like a taste of the UX, you can see a hi-fi static version of it here.
