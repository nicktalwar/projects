Windows 8

8/2008—5/2012, web services.

I planned, designed, and developed the UX and algorithms for search, type-ahead, app recommendations, and image processing web services for the Windows 8 Store (app store). A brilliant team of engineers and I built these services from the ground up for the diverse and large Windows user-base.

Working at Windows gave me the unique opportunity to understand mainstream consumer behavior at a detailed level, supported by statistically significant data. By thinking about tablets It set the stage for my education as an interaction designer by conceptualizing scenarios around tablets and mobile at the outset of their global adoption.
