Gridium

12/2012—6/2013, web.

Gridium applies advanced analytics to smart meter data to help cut energy costs.

Gridium and I began working together when their product was simply an email with energy management data and cost-cutting recommendations sent to property managers. To better serve their current and future customers, I was hired as a product designer to lead the conception and design of an energy management and cost-forecasting web product.

It was particularly challenging to distill complex energy management recommendations and data into accessible visualizations and a people-friendly UX. To this end, the final product has a mobile-like feel and intuitive drill-downs that progressively disclose data. Casual and enthusiast customers alike can now curb energy usage and cut costs across small and large properties.
