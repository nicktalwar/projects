Creating Chocolate Recipes to Pair with Philz Coffee

7/2013—9/2013, consumer confectionary.

Philz coffee is a Bay Area tradition. The coffeeshops dissolve into the neighborhoods they inhabit. Individually made, hand-poured coffee from over 30 different blends and roasts creates a unique and eclectic experience.

While mentoring a tech startup in Berkeley, the founder and I came-up with an idea to pair each Philz coffee blend, which range widely in taste, with a complimentary piece of dark chocolate. We dropped the startup and for the next few months, we learned how to make chocolate and came-up with 27 different recipe prototypes and winnowed it to five final coffee-chocolate combinations.

Interaction design is all about understanding human psychology to shape product to delight and provide value to people. Developing and testing chocolate prototypes firsthand enhanced my skills in a completely different medium, which was as fascinating as it was delicious.
