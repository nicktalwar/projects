Keepsake

5/2013—present, iOS.

Keepsake organizes your photos into stories with one tap. You can then edit and curate them as you desire.

The product goals of Keepsake are twofold. First, understand and enable people to organize content so that it represents memories vividly. Second, develop algorithms that can seed and support this interaction.

Over time, Keepsake will preserve all your content across devices in the cloud, semantically.
