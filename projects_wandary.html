Wandary

5/2013—7/2013, iOS.

Two personal stories inspired Wandary.

The first was a family road-trip vacation through the Okanagan Valley wine country in Canada. Faced with exorbitant roaming charges, yet still needing the ability to help my Dad navigate, I found myself taking screenshots of directions and maps on my iPad prior to driving. It was messy, to say the least.

The second was mentoring a startup called MapsWithMe. As the top startup out of Eastern Europe in a Europe-Silicon Valley startup accelerator exchange program, they had the best offline maps technology on all mobile platforms. Apparently the frustrations I had in Canada were common around the world as people had limited data plans and/or travelled.

Wandary took my problem and married it with MapsWithMe's industry-leading offline maps technology. The interaction enables people to create diaries as they are on their travels without service. Once on wifi, the app would upload the diaries to a service to preserve their memories.

Wandary's interactive prototype inspired and led to the overall iPad and iPhone startup project, allAway.
