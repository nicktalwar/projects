Urbandipity

11/2008—8/2011, web.

Urbandipity was a social web app that enabled people to meet-up easily based on common interests. I led product, business development, and marketing efforts among other things as a cofounder. Our launch attracted Seattle Twitter tastemakers in partnership with Seattle's largest publication, the Seattle Weekly.

Fed-up with the social scene right after college, my friends and I conceptualized Urbandipity as a place where people could easily find others near them with the same interests and meet-up. Over three years, we conceptualized the product, making successful pivots and surviving a cease-and-desist to reach product-market fit.
